Places I've travelled to in Australia

The Journey

Beginning in the urban setting of Melbourne, I first got my feet on the ground as an au pair in a nice neighbourhood in orderto familiarize myself with the country in the home of people who knew it best.

From Melbourne I was able to visit a couple of nearby areas such as Portsea, Torquay and the Great Ocean Road.

Following my au pair contract I went exploring more of the country outside of city life. Flying to Manly, Sydney for a short while to meet up with a friend and drive north.
